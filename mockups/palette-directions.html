Vertex · design exploration

Three palette directions

Same elevated Core layout, three different canvases. The big move is the stat triptych (the three Core Stats as oversized serif numerals carrying their colors), plus a warmer or cooler base than pure black. Tell me which world feels like Vertex, and I'll apply it to the real app. Each is built from swappable tokens, so the winner ports cleanly.
